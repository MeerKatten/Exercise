Daily Leg Flexibility & Strength Routine

‚è≥ Total time: ~30-40 minutes

1. Warm-up (5-7 min)

March in place 2 min
March on the spot, lifting knees high.
Leg swings (front & back) 30 sec per leg
Swing one leg forward and backward while maintaining balance.
Leg swings (side to side) 30 sec per leg
Swing one leg side to side while maintaining balance.
Hip circles 30 sec per direction
Rotate your hips in circles to loosen them up.

2. Mobility Drills (5-8 min)

Knee-to-wall ankle mobilization 10 reps per leg
Gently push knee forward towards the wall while keeping heel down.
90/90 hip stretch 30 sec per side
Sit with one leg forward at 90 degrees and the other back at 90 degrees. Lean forward gently.
Hip flexor stretch 30 sec per side
Kneel on one knee, push hips forward to stretch the hip flexor.
Calf stretch (against wall) 30 sec per side
Place hands on wall, step one foot back and press heel down.

3. Strength & Stability (10-15 min)

Wall squats 3 sets of 10 reps
Stand with back against a wall, lower into squat, then return to standing.
Goblet squats 3 sets of 10 reps
Hold a weight close to your chest and perform deep squats.
Chair-assisted squats 3 sets of 12 reps
Use a chair for support and squat slowly down and up.
Glute bridges 3 sets of 15 reps
Lie on back, lift hips towards ceiling, squeeze glutes.
Clamshells 2 sets of 15 reps per side
Lie on side, open and close knees while keeping feet together.
Bird-dog 2 sets of 10 reps per side
On hands and knees, extend opposite arm and leg simultaneously.
Plank 2 sets of 30 sec
Hold a straight plank position on elbows.

4. Flexibility & Cooldown (5-7 min)

Ankle dorsiflexion stretch 30 seconds
Stretch the front of the ankle by sitting back on feet.
Hip flexor stretch (again) 30 sec per side
Kneel on one knee, push hips forward.
Seated forward fold 30 sec
Sit with legs extended, reach forward to stretch hamstrings.
Seated hamstring stretch 30 sec per leg
Sit with one leg extended straight and the other bent, then gently lean forward over your extended leg, keeping your back straight.
Butterfly stretch 30 sec per leg
To do this stretch, you will sit on the ground, putting the soles of your feet together. You can hold either at your ankles or at your feet. And then gently lean forward until you feel stretched to the inside of your thighs. Hold the stretch for 30 seconds.
